Frequently Asked Questions

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations for parties of all sizes. You can make a reservation by calling us at (239) 471-7792 or by using the reservation form on our website's homepage.

Is there parking available?

Yes, we have a spacious parking lot available for our guests at no additional charge.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering for events of all sizes. Please contact us directly for catering menu options and pricing.

Are you available for private events?

Yes, we can accommodate private events and parties. Please contact us for more information about reserving space for your special occasion.

Do you offer vegetarian options?

Yes, we have several vegetarian dishes on our menu, and we are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions whenever possible.

Do you offer takeout or delivery?

Yes, we offer takeout orders. Please call us directly to place your order. We also partner with several delivery services in the Cape Coral area.
