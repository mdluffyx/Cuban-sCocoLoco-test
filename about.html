Our Story

Learn about the passion behind Cuban's Coco Loco

Our Journey

Cuban's Coco Loco was born from a love of authentic Cuban cuisine and a desire to share these flavors with the vibrant community of Cape Coral. Established on November 29th, 2024, our restaurant has become a beloved destination for those seeking a genuine taste of Cuba.

Our founder, Miguel Hernandez, brought generations of family recipes from Havana to Florida, preserving the traditional techniques and flavors that make Cuban cuisine so special. Every dish we serve is prepared with the same care and attention to detail that Miguel's grandmother used in her kitchen in Cuba.

At Cuban's Coco Loco, we believe that good food brings people together. Our mission is to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy delicious meals and make lasting memories.

Our Values

Authentic Flavor

We stay true to traditional Cuban recipes, using time-honored cooking methods and the freshest ingredients to deliver authentic flavors in every dish.

Family First

Our restaurant is family-owned and operated, and we treat our guests like extended family, welcoming everyone with open arms and warm hospitality.

Passion for Quality

We pour our hearts into everything we do, from selecting the finest ingredients to preparing each dish with care and attention to detail.

Community Connection

We are proud to be part of the Cape Coral community and strive to create a gathering place where neighbors become friends over delicious food and drinks.
